About me

A passion for music, a gift for teaching.

Hello! I'm Moshani Fernando — a classically trained pianist and dedicated music educator with 20 years of music knowledge and experience, and 15 years of high school teaching behind me.

I have been teaching privately and working with ABRSM, Trinity College of Music and ANZCA examination boards since 2007. I am very confident in guiding beginners and intermediate learners, and in bringing them to a higher level of musical performance.

Students have the wonderful opportunity to perform at the Dunedin Music Society, IRMTNZ concerts, Modern School of Music competitions, and ANZCA practical examinations.

DIP LCM · ATCL · LLCM — London College of Music, University of West London

What I offer

Classes for every stage of your journey.

From tiny fingers to seasoned hands — there's a place for every learner at SoundSteps Piano.

Little Pianists
Ages 7 – 10

A playful, imaginative introduction to the piano. Young learners develop rhythm, coordination, and a love for music through games and songs.

  • 20-minute sessions tailored to young learners
  • Note reading through fun visual methods
  • Finger technique & posture foundations
  • Simple, recognisable pieces to build confidence

Junior Pianists
Ages 10 – 17

Structured lessons that build real skill — from scales and theory to exam preparation and recital repertoire.

  • 30-minute weekly lessons
  • ABRSM exam preparation (Grades 1–8)
  • Music theory integrated into every lesson
  • Classical, pop & contemporary repertoire
  • Annual student recital opportunities

Never too late to start, never too advanced to grow. Flexible lessons built around your schedule, goals, and musical interests.
